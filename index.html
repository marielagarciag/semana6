What is visual communication?

Visual Communication is the transmission of information and ideas using symbols and imagery. It's one of three main types of communication:

  1. Verbal communication (speaking)
  2. Non verbal communication (tone, body language, etc.)
Visual communication is believed to be the type that people rely one the most, and it includes:
  • Signs
  • Graphic Designs
  • Films
  • Others

Why visuals are so important

The concepts of graphics stick in the person's head much more easily than words. The target audience will see the graphics and immediately acquire new knowledge because of them. Images and video convey a richer experience than text-heavy content alone.
In a media landscape filled with clutter, readers' attention is hard to acquire, and even harder to retain. It ensures that a clear, unified message is delivered.
